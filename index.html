AUTISM (Autism Spectrum Disorder)

Autism, also known as Austism Spectrum Disorder, are several types of disorders caused by the brain functioning diffrently then usual, which can lead to social and behavioral challenges. It can affect people of all ages or in other words its an spectrum disorder.

Main symptoms of Autism include:
  • Difficulty of comphrehending feelings
  • Feeling uneasy about social situations
  • Not liking to be around people or making friends
